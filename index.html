Mad Scientist

Story

In the high-security laboratory for free energy research, the unthinkable has happened: enemies have infiltrated the lab!

At the last second, the brilliant but mad professor – the Mad Scientist – manages to grab a lightning generator. Now it’s up to you to defend the lab with brains and skill!

Collect energy balls to unleash powerful supershots and keep your life up with hearts. Use bombs to take out enemies faster, and manage your ammo wisely so you’re ready for the final boss.

Every collected energy ball increases your score – show what you’re made of and save the future of energy!

Controls

  • Move Left: left
  • Move Right: right
  • Jump: up
  • Shoot: Y
  • Bomb: D
  • Superlaser: S
  • Pause: P
